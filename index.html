Tech + Accessibility + Design

As an Information Systems student at Carnegie Mellon University, my work and studies lie at the intersection of these principles. Through my classes, I have been taught to locate real issues faced by real people and work towards solving them using technology.

My goal with my websites is to create user experiences which are clear and concise for any user, no matter what level of digital literacy while putting my own creative spin on it :).

About Me

I've always had a lifelong passion for art and design. Coding crept into my life later, when in Middle School I got my first taste of programming through Scratch and Python. Though rudimentary, I was fascinated by the potential of Scratch to teach anyone how to code through a easy to understand and visually appealing interface.

While I've learned much more about coding and design since my Scratch Era, that initial spark is still there, and it's what drives me to learn more about the constantly changing worlds of code and design.

It's always been my dream to do something I love as a career, and it's a goal I continue pursuing everyday.
