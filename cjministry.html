Context.

CJ Ministry is a local Pittsburgh nonprofit aiming to form community through service. Specifically, they work with local organizations to help address Homelessness, Elder Abuse, and Drug Abuse.

I worked directly with Candace, CJ Ministry's founder in Fall of 2023 to help them design a website from scratch as at the time, they only had a Facebook page, making it very hard for volunteers to get information or offer their time or monetary support.

We went through brainstorming, lo-fi wireframes, hi-fi wireframes, and multiple rounds of user testing in order to develop this website. Though at the time it was the first full website I had built with an actual client, I look back fondly as it was a project which was extremely involved and meaningful to me.
